As a music production company that specializes in creating music for fashion shows and walkthroughs, we take pride in offering a wide range of services to our clients. Here are some of the key items about our services

Pre-made sets of tracks with a one-time license 

This is a convenient and cost-effective option for clients who want to use pre-made sets of tracks at their show without worrying about the legal and licensing issues that come with using copyrighted music.

 
Artist performance (Flowers 15)

This option adds a live element to the show, which can be exciting and engaging for the audience. Our team of skilled musicians can perform a variety of music styles to match the theme of the show.
Instrumental set in the form of a playback

This option is great for clients who want to save money on hiring live musicians or who want more control over the music played at their show. We ensure that the quality of the playback is high enough to meet the expectations of the audience and the client.
Turnkey creation of the style and atmosphere of the show

This option requires a high level of creativity and skill from our music production team. We work closely with our clients to create a unique and memorable experience for the audience by creating a sound and composition that perfectly matches the style and atmosphere of the show.
